Pepperoni Pizza

Description

This pepperoni pizza recipe produces a quick and easy classic! Delicious homemade pizza crust and tomato sauce have never been easier.

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 12 ounce CONTADINA Tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Dough

  • 3 1/4 flour
  • .25 ounce pizza yeast
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/3 cups warm water
  • 1/3 cup oil

Toppings

  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 6 ounce pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425F
  2. Whisk sauce ingredients until smooth
  3. Make crust: Combine flour, yeast, sugar, and salt in large bowl. Add warm water and oil until well blended.
  4. Knead dough
  5. Flatten dough onto pizza pan
  6. Top dough with sauce, cheese, and pepperoni
  7. Bake 18 to 20 minutes until golden.
